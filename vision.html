How To Vote

For NJIT

My vision for NJIT is to transform our campus into one that is more socially engaging and gives students the opportunity grow personally, professionally, and academically. I want to create an environment that encourages students to be themselves and allows them to be immersed in communities filled with similar people.

For Clubs

Clubs have enormous potential for growth at NJIT. My vision for clubs is to turn them into leader-developing machine. I want clubs to give their members the opportunity to develop strong skills in professionalism and encourage them to aim to make a strong impact on the NJIT community.

For Senate

My vision for Senate is to transform it into a group of distinguished students that are passionate about making a positive impact at NJIT. In order to successfully govern such a vibrant community, we need leaders who are willing to sacrifice some sleep and spare time for the greater good. That is a Senate we can truly appreciate.

For Students

Being involved is a great way to enjoy your experience at NJIT. My vision for students is to keep them informed and give them the opportunity to make an impact on campus. We can make a difference if we all remain informed and stand up for what is beneficial for us.
